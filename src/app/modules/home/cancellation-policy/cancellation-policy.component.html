Cancellation policy

Orastays protects the interest of both the host and the guest. The cancellation policy applies to special circumstances. You must read the cancellation policy before making your reservation. Cancellation policy of each listing will differ from the other and each will have their own policy. Guests will be able to read the cancellation policy of the listing once they are proceeding towards the reservation of a property. Cancellation on long-term reservations of more than 28 night will have different policies that too will be fixed with the concerned listing.

Short term booking:
  • Bookings cancelled 60 days before the stay will receive a full 100% refund
  • Bookings cancelled from the 60th day to the 30th day of the start of stay will get a 75% refund of their total booking charged
  • Booking cancelled from the 30th day to the 15 days of the start of stay will get 50% refund of the total booking charged
  • Booking cancelled from the 30th day to the 15 days of the start of stay will get 50% refund of the total booking charged
  • Booking cancelled from the 30th day to the 15 days of the start of stay will get 50% refund of the total booking charged
  • After the 7th day, the guest will not receive any refund upon cancellation
  • If the guest arrives in the property but wishes to leave within 24 hrs. The first night fee will be deducted, and the rest will be returned. However, if the time period exceeds then no refund will be provided
Long Term Booking:

If a guest books a long-term stay and cancels a reservation before the start date, the booking amount for one full month will have to be paid to the host.

After arriving at the property, the guest decides to leave regardless of the checkout date, the guest will need to pay for 30 days following the cancellation date or whatever agreement had been settled with the host during reservation.Â 

In each case of cancellation, the guest will not receive a refund of the GST and the commission amount.
